Our Processing Lines

Overview of Our 9 Cleaning & Grading Lines

We operate 9 high-performance seed processing lines used to clean and grade sesame, peanuts, and other grains. These machines handle 3–8 tons per hour and deliver export-quality products with less than 2% impurities.

Step-by-Step Process

Loading: Seeds poured into the top hopper.

Air Cleaning: Suction fans remove dust, husks, and straw.

Vibration & Screening: Vibrating sieves separate debris and size the seeds.

Grading: Clean seeds sorted into size grades.

Stone Separation: Gravity tables remove heavy impurities.

Final Output: Clean seeds ready for export or color sorting.

Advanced Color Sorting Line (6SXZ)

Our color sorter line integrates seed cleaning, destoning, and advanced camera-based color sorting to ensure perfect quality. It processes 3–5 tons/hour and is ideal for final export preparation.

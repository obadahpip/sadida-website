The Uniqueness of Mixed Sesame

Our mixed sesame is sourced directly from Chad’s fertile farmlands, where sesame cultivation has been perfected for generations. By combining the purity and oil richness of white sesame with the earthy flavor and strength of brown sesame, Sadida creates a product that is versatile, nutritious, and export-ready.

Each batch is processed through our advanced cleaning and grading, ensuring consistency and less than 2% impurities—meeting the highest international standards.

Why Sadida Mixed Sesame Stands Out

  • Balanced Blend – The ideal mix of white and brown sesame to satisfy diverse market needs.
  • Premium Quality – Cleaned and sorted with cutting-edge technology for purity and uniformity.
  • Versatility – Perfect for snacks, tahini, oil extraction, bakery products, and more.
  • Trusted Supply – Large-scale capacity and reliable delivery networks from Chad to the world.

The Purity of White Sesame

Sadida white sesame seeds are harvested from trusted local farms across Chad, a region globally recognized for producing some of the world’s finest sesame. What sets our sesame apart is its exceptional purity, rich oil content, and uniform size. These attributes make it the first choice for leading buyers in the Middle East, Asia, and Europe.

Every seed goes through Sadida’s advanced cleaning and sorting lines, equipped with the latest color-sorting and grading technology. This ensures sesame that meets international export standards with less than 2% impurities—a benchmark that few can achieve.

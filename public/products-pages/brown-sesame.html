The Strength of Brown Sesame

Sadida Brown Sesame is carefully sourced from local farms in Chad, where traditional farming practices and fertile soil create sesame of outstanding quality. Known for its robust flavor, high oil yield, and nutritional benefits, it is widely used in oil extraction, tahini, snacks, and bakery products.

Each batch is processed through our advanced cleaning, grading, and color-sorting lines, ensuring purity and less than 2% impurities, making Sadida Brown Sesame export-ready for the most demanding markets.
